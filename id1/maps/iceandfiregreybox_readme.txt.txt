Description             : Welcome to the QuakeLevelDesign wiki! This Map is called Fire and Ice. The goal of this map is to try to kill many people as you can. the first person or team killed 50 people win the game. There are two normal weapons on both sides, those place can be a win or lose, it would help the game, but not that powerful. The most powerful weapon is in the center of the map this weapon may affect the whole game. Watch for the lava or the water down the bottom. Players can hide or die in there.
